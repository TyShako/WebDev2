Overview On Early Gaming

When developers created the very first games they never could’ve imagined it to expand to such high levels. Games in the early days were different, not only in the teams that were building them but also in the resources they could use. Throughout the 60s-80s gaming was limited to small and basic designs. In the 80s however, gaming evolved into a much bigger product with the start of arcade gaming and the audience that those systems brought. Arcades brought along with them a new culture of going out, playing games with some friends and just hanging out. Yet, as you will see, the 1990s was the revolution that gaming so desperately needed.
