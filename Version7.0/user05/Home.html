A Brief Overview on Gaming

For generations gaming has brought communities together into a shared space with common interests. Every-single-day most people interact with games in forms like Smartphones, PC, or when you don’t have WIFI (Dinosaur game). To continue, the influence gaming has had on American society is exponential, with the very first gaming event (Spacewar) to the most recent influencer like Ninja hosting the ball drop at Times Square. Overall, gaming continues to evolve with the times and will continue to for the foreseeable future.
